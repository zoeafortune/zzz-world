this is a found poem; writing poetry is not my strong suit but maybe if i write this in a cool script font i'll look like shakespeare.

sitting inside
grasping a void
spinning in space
 flowers under the roof
nuclear light bulb
spring festival of dance
red rays
reflections on your palm
chickens in the sun
leaves through the window
ladder through the roof
reaching to the sky
giant dragonfly army
fire at the waterpark
no claustrophobia for tiny flamingos
palm on your back
suspended in mid air
...
